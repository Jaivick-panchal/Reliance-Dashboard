# Reliance Industries — Stock Performance Dashboard 📊

An interactive Power BI dashboard built on 2 years of real NSE stock data for Reliance Industries (RELIANCE.NS).

## Tools Used
- Python, Pandas, yfinance — data extraction & cleaning
- Power BI — dashboard design & visualization

## Dashboard Features
- 📌 KPI Cards — Peak Price, Lowest Price, Average Price, Current Price
- 📈 Line Chart — Closing price with 20-day & 50-day Moving Averages
- 📊 Volume Chart — Daily trading volume (NSE) across 450+ trading days
- 🔘 Year Slicer — Filter entire dashboard by 2024, 2025, 2026

## Key Insights
- Reliance peaked at ₹1,580 in Jul 2024 and again in Jan 2026
- Death Cross in Oct 2024 predicted the crash to ₹1,150 by Apr 2025
- Golden Cross in mid-2025 signaled the recovery back to ₹1,580
- Average price over 2 years: ₹1,370
